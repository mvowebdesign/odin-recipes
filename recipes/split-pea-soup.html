Split Pea Soup

Description

This soup is a great winter-warmer. The recipe is simple to make and is rich in proteins and fibres.

Ingredients

  • 360g split peas
  • 2 onions
  • basil
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chicken stock (1 cube)
  • water

Steps

  1. Dice the onions and cook them in a pot with some oil and some salt.
  2. Rinse the split peas.
  3. Once the onions are cooked, add the split peas and the chicken stock cube.
  4. Cover with a large amount of water.
  5. Bring to the boil then cook for about 30 minutes until the split peas are tender.
  6. Strain and keep the cooking water.
  7. Add some basil and whizz into desired consistency, adding back some cooking water if necessary.
