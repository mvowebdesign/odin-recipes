Tarte Tatin

Description

The Tarte Tatin is a french classic. With loads of butter and sugar that caramelise around the apples, it will satiate any craving for sweets!

Ingredients

  • 250g sugar
  • 100g butter
  • 6 apples
  • 1 puff pastry

Steps

  1. Peel and core the apples and cut them in half.
  2. In a cake pan that goes on the stove and in the oven, place the sugar and the butter.
  3. Place the apples on top of the sugar and butter, their convex side towards the bottom.
  4. Cover with the puff pastry, tucking the extra pastry inside the pan.
  5. Pierce small hole in the middle of the puff pastry to allow for the hot air to escape and the puff pastry to not bloat too much.
  6. Place on medium heat on the stove until the sugar and butter caramelise slightly (about 1/4h). Be careful not to let the caramel go too dark; check very regularly.
  7. Once the caramel is ready, place the pan in the oven preheated at 190 degrees Celsius for about 20 min.
  8. Once golden, remove from the oven, let it cool slightly and then flip onto serving plate.
